30+ years of building extraordinary outdoor experiences via unique accommodations and providing outstanding wilderness services.

We are in the business of Mindful Outdoor Adventure & Recreation.

Some recreate to conquer while others recreate to be conquered. Central to everything we offer, from luxury camping to custom outdoor adventures, is the understanding of life as an interdependent phenomenon necessitating attentiveness, knowledge, respect and humility. Our greatest joy is in providing sustainable recreational experiences that not only yield momentary happiness but also lifelong insight and wisdom. We are driven by a passion to inspire others and offer soul moving experiences that change people’s lives and improve the world.

A MONTANA BIODYNAMIC FARM EXPERIENCE

Food Forests, Permaculture, Regenerative Agriculture. Heard of it? You will. Scientists, farmers, and people around the world are catching on. We are embarking on an expedition to create a small scale farm experience that embraces these principles and provide a legacy of abundance for generations to come. A place for education, experiential farm stays, and farm to fork nutrient dense food is our goal.
