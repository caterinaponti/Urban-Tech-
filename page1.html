USF Student guide to sustainable UrbanTech living!

Our definition of UrbanTech

We wanted to create a user-friendly website in order to help our peers promote a sustainable living through the idea of walking to places near our school. These selective places are carefully chosen based on their distance from the center point, which is our school.

WHY WALK?

We believe that living in a city where necessities are connected by distance, we should implement walking to these places. It allows for a sustainable enviornment which embodies urbantech values.
